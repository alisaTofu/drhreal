Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how DrHorrible.com collects, uses, and protects information when you visit the official site for Dr. Horrible’s Sing-Along Blog.

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

When we say “we”, “us” or “our” in this Privacy Policy, we mean the operators of DrHorrible.com. We run this site so fans of Dr. Horrible’s Sing-Along Blog can learn more about the musical, explore official products, and discover web extras from the lab.

This page describes what information may be collected when you visit the website, why we collect it, and what choices you have. By using the site, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you choose to provide

We may collect information you provide directly to us, for example when you:

  • send us a message through a contact form;
  • request information about products or web extras;
  • sign up to receive occasional news or updates (if available);
  • communicate with us by email or other channels.

The information you provide may include your name, email address, and any other details you choose to share in your message.

2.2 Information collected automatically

When you visit DrHorrible.com, some information is collected automatically by your browser or device, such as:

  • IP address and approximate location (city/country level);
  • browser type and settings;
  • device type and operating system;
  • pages you visit, time spent, and referring URLs.

We use this information to understand how visitors use the site and to keep DrHorrible.com secure and reliable.

2.3 Cookies and similar technologies

Like most websites, we may use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and to measure site performance. Some cookies are strictly necessary for the site to function, while others help us understand which pages are most interesting to visitors.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above for purposes such as:

  • operating, maintaining, and improving DrHorrible.com;
  • responding to your messages and requests;
  • understanding how fans interact with pages, products, and web extras;
  • detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues;
  • complying with legal obligations and enforcing our site terms.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

If you are located in a region that requires a legal basis for processing (such as the European Economic Area or United Kingdom), we process personal information on the basis of:

  • Consent, when you choose to contact us or opt in to receive communications;
  • Legitimate interests, such as operating and improving the website, and keeping it secure;
  • Legal obligations, when we need to retain certain information for record-keeping.

5. When We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • trusted service providers who host the website, provide analytics, or help us operate the site (under contracts that require them to protect your information);
  • authorities or other parties when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights or the safety of others;
  • another organization in connection with a business transfer relating to the site (for example, a merger or reorganization).

6. Data Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as it is reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. When information is no longer needed, we take steps to delete or anonymize it.

7. Your Choices & Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights over your personal information, such as the right to:

  • request access to the information we hold about you;
  • ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete details;
  • request deletion of certain information;
  • object to or restrict specific types of processing;
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details in the “How to Contact Us” section below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

8. Third-Party Services & Links

DrHorrible.com may link to external services such as digital stores, social platforms, or streaming providers. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for how they handle your information. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any site you visit through a link from this one.

9. Children’s Privacy

DrHorrible.com is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent age of consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps to remove it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the site, legal requirements, or how we handle information. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Policy regularly to stay informed.
